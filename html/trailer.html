Clever Story and Great Character!

January 1, 2021 by Colin Krawchuk

I have been a fan of The Jester short films since the first one appeared on YouTube in 2016. The Jester himself has always been mysterious which allows the viewer to untangle the character in their own way. Now, seven years later, that character gets his own full length film written by the original creators of the YouTube shorts. I was lucky enough to be one of the first people to see the movie on the big screen, and I am extremely grateful for that opportunity.
